## 1. Industry Research

### Home Catering Market (Gading Serpong & Greater Tangerang)

**Market Overview:**
- Indonesia catering industry: estimated Rp 78 Trillion/year (2024), dominated by home-based caterers in suburban areas
- Gading Serpong / BSD belt: dense residential compound = high demand for catering (arisan, syukuran, ulang tahun, corporate)
- Home-based caterers have significant trust advantage: "masakan rumahan" = perceived healthier, more personal
- WA groups and ibu-ibu networks are the #1 discovery channel for home caterers
- Event catering (50–200 pax) is the highest-margin segment

**Industry Trends:**
- Digital shift: ibu-ibu increasingly Google "catering Gading Serpong" before asking in WA groups
- Photo-first selling: Food photography is the primary trust signal — no photo = no order
- Healthy food premium: Catering with "bahan segar" messaging commands 20–30% price premium
- Corporate lunch box is the fastest-growing segment (remote work → office return = corporate catering demand)
- Prasmanan culture: Javanese / Sundanese cuisine still dominates event catering

**Competitive Landscape:**
- Most home caterers: no website, only WA group presence + Instagram
- Key differentiators: Responsive, consistent quality, on-time delivery
- Very few have professional microsites → first mover wins digital discovery

**Pain Points (Customer):**
- "Mau pesan catering tapi ga tau harganya, harus tanya dulu"
- "Takut ga enak / porsinya kurang"
- "Ribet order, ga jelas prosedurnya"
- "Foto di WA/IG jelek, ga kebayang enaknya"

## 5. Revenue / Credibility / Exposure Strategy

**How this microsite drives revenue:**
1. **Direct Order Funnel:** Menu + harga jelas → "Pesan via WA" button → zero friction
2. **Corporate Discovery:** Google "catering nasi box Gading Serpong" → first impression yang profesional
3. **Social Proof:** Testimoni customer + galeri foto makanan → trust builder yang powerful
4. **Event Upsell:** Showcase paket prasmanan → customer yang tadinya cuma mau nasi box jadi upgrade ke event catering
5. **WOM Amplifier:** Pelanggan punya link resmi untuk share ke grup WA → referral yang mudah dan terukur
6. **Price Transparency:** Tidak ada lagi tanya-tanya panjang → konversi lebih cepat

**Expected Impact:**
- 50%+ reduction in pre-order inquiry time (harga sudah jelas di website)
- Corporate channel terbuka: Rp 15M–30M/bulan potensi dari satu klien corporate saja
- Prasmanan event inquiries meningkat 3x karena showcase capability lebih jelas
