## 1. Industry Research (Life & Health Insurance)

- **Pain Points:**
  - Complexity in choosing the right policy for wealth preservation and health.
  - Fear of claim rejection or slow service.
  - Lack of trust with hit-and-run agents; clients want a long-term advisor.
- **Opportunity:**
  - Positioning as a "Premium Protection Partner."
  - Using a modern, contentful, and highly authentic digital presence to build instant trust before the first meeting.

## 2. Offer Architecture

- **Executive Health Shield:** Premium medical coverage with VIP hospital access globally.
- **Legacy Preservation Plan:** Asset protection and inheritance planning for the next generation.
- **USP:** "Not just selling policies, but designing your family's financial fortress." High-touch, personalized service and claim advocacy from Tom and Eric.

## 3. Design Direction

- **Archetype:** Premium, Trustworthy, Authentic, Modern.
- **Palette:** Prudential Red (subtle accents), Deep Navy/Charcoal (trust), Warm White/Silver (premium feel).
- **Layout:** High-end editorial style. Clean typography, confident whitespace, strong and authentic imagery of Tom/Eric advising clients.
- **Tone:** Professional, Secure, Empathetic, Authoritative.

## 4. Key Sections for Microsite

1. **Hero Section:** Strong value proposition focusing on peace of mind and wealth protection.
2. **The "Why Us" (Authenticity):** Introduction to Tom and Eric's philosophy.
3. **Core Solutions:** Visual cards for Health Shield and Legacy Planning.
4. **Social Proof (Trust):** Testimonials or case study metrics from middle-to-upper class clients.
5. **Call to Action:** Invitation for an exclusive, no-obligation wealth protection audit.
