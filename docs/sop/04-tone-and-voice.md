# SOP 04: Tone & Voice (Copy Standards)

**Standard Operating Procedure for Karya Kilat Microsite Projects**

---

## 1. Persona Voice Map
Setiap niche harus punya cara bicara (voice) yang spesifik:

| Niche | Tone | Voice / Style | Kata Kunci |
|---|---|---|---|
| **High-End / Luxury** | Formal, Confident | "Professional Partner" | *Precision, Exclusive, Strategic, Fortress.* |
| **Tech-Forward** | Direct, Bold | "The Builder" | *Ship, Scale, Shipped, MVP, Code, Performance.* |
| **Artisan / Coffee** | Sensory, Warm | "The Storyteller" | *Ritual, Third Place, Community, Slow, Sourced.* |
| **Homey / Service** | Personal, Friendly | "Trusted Friend" | *Sayang, Keluarga, Rumahan, Segar, Higienis.* |

## 2. Headlines Strategy
Jangan buat headline yang "boring". Gunakan *Benefit-Driven Headlines*:
*   **Boring:** "Jasa Pembuatan Aplikasi Mobile"
*   **Karya Kilat (Tech):** "I Build Mobile Apps That Actually Work."
*   **Karya Kilat (Luxury):** "Protect Your Wealth. Eliminate Tax Anxiety."
*   **Karya Kilat (Homey):** "Rasa Sayang di Setiap Masakan."

## 3. The "2-Second" Hook (Hero Section)
Klien harus tahu 3 hal dalam 2 detik pertama:
1.  **Siapa kamu?** (Nama/Brand)
2.  **Apa yang kamu jual?** (Value Proposition)
3.  **Harus ngapain selanjutnya?** (CTA)

## 4. Copy Rules
*   **Pilih Aktif vs Pasif:** Gunakan kalimat aktif ("Kami masak tiap pagi") daripada pasif ("Masakan dimasak tiap pagi").
*   **Hapus Filler:** Hindari "Sangat", "Mungkin", "Coba-coba". Langsung to-the-point.
*   **Pahami Masalah (Pain Points):** Sebutkan masalah yang klien hadapi (e.g. "Takut kena audit", "Takut nasi box basi").
*   **Benefit > Feature:** Jangan cuma bilang "Ada WiFi", tapi bilang "Bisa WFH dengan WiFi kencang tanpa batas".
*   **Social Proof:** Gunakan kutipan asli klien (jika ada) atau buat testimoni yang terdengar manusiawi (bukan robot).
